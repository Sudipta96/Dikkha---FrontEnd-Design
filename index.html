Preamble

Punch line of Preamble

Whatever work we do, we focus our vision

Dikkha Skill Development (DSD) is delighted to take the opportunity to make a strong footprint in the journey towards ‘Skilled Bangladesh’. The opportunity will reflect collectively on the current youth employment situation, implementing practical programs, strategies and empower the youth to participate more meaningfully in the economic development of the country.

Skill development encompasses several vital attributes, including identifying one's skill gaps and enabling one to develop those skills to achieve their goal.

What We are

What we are punchline here

Dikkha Skill Development (DSD) has started it’s journey in June 2020 as an online training platform. Since then, we have been providing training to individuals on Microsoft Excel. We have also performed numerous corporate training programs on Microsoft Excel as part of our distinctive and dynamic training methodologies and delivery.

Many officials from prestigious companies or Conglomerate Groups turn to us for our expertise to solve their Microsoft Excel problems. And, we have been effective in giving them consulting services on how to manage official MIS reporting and Data Visualization. We are slowly but sustainably an emerging firm in Bangladesh.

We have strengthened our portfolio with the inclusion of Computer Skills, Communications Skills and Numerous Soft Skills. Now we have emerged with the facilities of online and offline trainings for the youth, specially the university pass outs who are preparing themselves to enter in the competitive market of professionals.

We are destined to help this segment towards achieving their goals. Your organization may avail all the required services from Dikkha Skill Development (DSD) to equip your ambassadors better.

Our Aim & Objectives

Aim & Objectives Tagline

  • We aim to serve as career mentors for job seeking individuals by identifying their needs and addressing those requirements necessary to enhance their skill set.
  • To prepare senior students, graduates, and job seekers for their desired careers with crucial soft and hard skills and communication skills through our skill development programs.
  • To provide students with the services they need in order to pursue their selected professional path.
  • To support students who want to be entrepreneurs in every way with the aid of our skill-development program.
  • To be able to minimize the gap between the employer and their ideal candidates, we strive to provide students with the necessary skill sets and make them the best fit for the appropriate role.
  • To support students who want to be entrepreneurs in every way with the aid of our skill-development program.
